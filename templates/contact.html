Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unopened products. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. We also offer express shipping (2-3 days) and international shipping options. All orders are tracked for your convenience.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Yes! All Valora Beauty products are cruelty-free and formulated without testing on animals. We're committed to ethical beauty practices.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Absolutely! We offer complimentary luxury gift wrapping for all orders. You can add a personalized gift message during checkout.
